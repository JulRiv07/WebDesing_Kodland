The best anti-stress toy — endless bubble popping fun!

What is Pop It?

Pop-it literally means “pop it”. It is a children's toy that works similar to bubble wrap. However, unlike bubble wrap, pop-its can be pressed infinitely. The silicone bubbles simply flip to the other side creating a satisfying popping feeling and sound.

Game rules

Players take turns pressing bubbles. You must finish the row you start. You can press up to three bubbles at once and they must be next to each other. The goal is to make your opponent press the last bubble. Whoever presses the last bubble loses.
